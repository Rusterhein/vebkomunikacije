About Pokémon

Pokémon are creatures of various shapes and sizes who inhabit the Pokémon world. The world of Pokemon is inhabited by creatures known as Pokemon, which humans, known as Pokemon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport. The main goal for many Trainers is to become a Pokemon Master by capturing and training a diverse team of Pokemon and defeating other Trainers in battles.
